Event information and Rules. REL

The tournament will follow MTG Competitive REL, and certified judges will be present. It will also adhere to the European cEDH guidelines, which can be consulted online https://juizes-mtg-portugal.github.io/.

Proxies & Decklists: Each deck may include proxies for all Reserved List cards

plus additional proxies for non-Reserved List cards.So there is no limit. Proxies must have recognizable Magic: The Gathering artwork and be distinguishable from real cards, such as having a different back. They must be double-sleeved, and paper printouts will not be allowed. Decklists are mandatory and must be submitted on our Registration site via a Moxfield link or printed on paper before the check in starts.

Trajanje runde: 90 min (Round Duration: 90 min) Rounds

NEMA EXTRA POTEZA (THERE ARE NO EXTRA TURNS) When time is called out, active player may finish the turn normally. If any stagnation or wasted time is noticed during the final turn, the round will automatically end as a draw by the judge's decision

If player A is eliminated and game ends in a draw, all players including player A will get a point by the official rules.Points in a draw

Seating order will be set automatically, as well as the Player (starting) order

In Multiplayer Tournaments, it is encouraged for Players that wish to concede a Game, to do so at any time they could play a sorcery and when stack is empty.

Point system

A Win awards 5 points, A Draw awards 1 point (for all Players except those incurring harsh penalties - Game Loss, Match Loss, Disqualification). A Loss awards 0 points 5/1/0 system. We will not have byes, instead there will be pod of 3 players in case of a number of participants not divisible by 4. The matchmaking will be random pairings.

The entry fee is 30€ and must be paid on-site.

When arriving at the event, you MUST mention that you are here for the MTG tournament. And you will be directed to the playing venue, where the entrance fee will be collected

Please verify your decklists via Moxfield or print them on paper.

The prizes were formed and based on 28 players.

Head Judge: Borna Atlagić

Our Judges are here to help you! If you notice anything that seems shady, odd, or out of place, please call a Judge. If you have any rules questions, please call a Judge
